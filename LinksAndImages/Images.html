Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos. They keep file sizes small, but they don't have transparent pixels so they are not as high quality as a png image.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations my life.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. They do not limit the colour palette so they show images more faithfully. This also means that they are larger than JPGs

SVGs

These are vector based images so they scale really well. Their issue is that they need any text fields to be converted to outlines.
